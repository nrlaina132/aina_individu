My life, My journey |December 1, 2020

Hello everyone! Thank you for visiting to my website. Here I would like to share my life and journey with you guys. My name is Nurul Aina binti Ismail. I am 20 years old and originally from Negeri Sembilan. I am a final year student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) in Rembau campus. I majored in Diploma in Information Management.

Living as a student and also a child to your parents is not an easy thing. Every child has their own responsibility to their parents while as a student they are responsible to seek for knowledge. There are those who are struggling as they grow up. They have been through so many things while growing up and that is what makes them more mature. At the end of the day, we will realize that we are ready to face the world full of challenges and upside down of life.

#FoodHunter

The Sharing Plate |January 27, 2020

The Sharing Plates is one of the most popular cafes in the Melaka area. The location of this cafe is located at Jalan Bendahara opposite to Hotel Ramada Plaza by Whyndam Melaka. Sharing Plates cafe is a franchise for The Daily Fix Cafe located on Jalan Hang Jebat not far from Jalan Bendahara. At that time, I was with my cousin having breakfast at this cafe. We arrived at 8 o'clock and the cafe was already full, so we decided to wait for an empty table. The menu at Sharing Plates is very tasty especially their soft pancakes. My cousin ordered their French Toast with Teh Tarik Sauce and it tastes so yummy! We really did enjoy their food and I can't wait to be there again with my cousin when I return to my hometown.
